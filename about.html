About

Gender pay gap is worse than thought: The World Economic Forum's Global Gender Gap Report 2018 shows women actually earn half the income of men in Georgia.

Gender Gap GE, funded by civicSignal is a solution designed to advocate healthy labor market conditions. The website represents to help people calculate the gender pay gap to see the differences visually and stimulate critical thinking on this issue.

The tool uses Estimated Earned Income data from The World Economic Forum's Global Gender Gap Report 2018. Gender Gap here represents the gap between men and women in pay.

The project is initiated by Code for Africaand redefined and implemented by Civic Engagements Reserch and Design Center for Georgia.

The code is available on GitHub. You are free to reuse it to help empower your own community.
